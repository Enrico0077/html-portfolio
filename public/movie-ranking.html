The best Al Pacino movies according to Henry Amponsah

My Top 3 favourite movies.

The Godfather Part II

Al Pacino delivers a masterful performance in The Godfather Part II, portraying Michael Corleone with chilling intensity and emotional depth. He seamlessly conveys Michael’s transformation into a ruthless, isolated leader, blending quiet menace with moments of vulnerability, making it one of his most iconic roles.

Dog Day Afternoon

In Dog Day Afternoon, Al Pacino gives a raw and electrifying performance as Sonny, a desperate, unpredictable bank robber. His portrayal captures a perfect balance of vulnerability, charisma, and volatility, making the character both compelling and deeply human.

Scarface

Al Pacino delivers a larger-than-life performance in Scarface as Tony Montana, exuding fierce ambition and unrelenting intensity. He masterfully portrays Tony’s rise and fall, blending explosive charisma with moments of chilling brutality, creating one of cinema's most iconic antiheroes.
